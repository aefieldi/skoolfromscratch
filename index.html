💬 Top Questions
How much content should go in your free community? ▼
Enough to demonstrate value and keep people engaged — but NOT so much that they never need anything else. Your free community should give them a taste of your methodology and a clear path to get more support. The Freemium model works best: free community gives access, premium unlocks the real goods.
Should I start free and convert to paid later, or keep free as the lead magnet? ▼
The Freemium model is the move. Keep a free community as the lead magnet and create a separate paid community (or paid tier) as the upsell inside the free one. This is how you build a consistent pipeline without constantly running ads.
Do you recommend starting free if you don't have a following? ▼
Yes — 100%. Free removes the friction barrier and lets you build trust, get feedback, and validate your offer before asking for money. Once you have testimonials and engagement, converting to a paid model is way easier.
How do you onboard members properly? ▼
The golden onboarding sequence: (1) Auto-DM with welcome + CTA, (2) Personalized Loom video within 24 hours showing them around and booking an onboarding call, (3) Point them to the Start Here module, (4) Add them to your weekly call calendar. The goal is to get them to their first win FAST.
Public or private community? ▼
Free communities usually do better public — anyone can stumble in, post and see value, and feel the vibe before joining. Private makes sense for paid communities or when you want a more curated, intimate space.
How do you attract your first 100 members? ▼
Three lanes: (1) Personal outreach — go through every contact, email list, and follower and send personal invites. (2) Content — start your YouTube/IG/TikTok engine pointing back to the community. (3) Cross-community — show up in other communities (like Spiritual Business Skool!) and provide value. Organic + personal is the fastest path early on.
How do you add coaching to a free community? ▼
Add locked content at Level 3 (weekly call recordings, mini-courses). Then offer a premium tier or a separate paid community with live coaching calls. The free community qualifies leads; the coaching offer converts them.
